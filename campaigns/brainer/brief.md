# Campaign Brief — North Indian Agency Partnership Outreach

## Objective

Build partnership relationships with **North Indian software development agencies, design agencies, and implementation companies** that already work with **US clients** and have an established delivery pipeline.

The goal is not traditional lead generation.

The goal is:

> position FusionSync AI as a **technical implementation partner** that helps agencies expand the services they can confidently offer.

---

## Positioning

FusionSync AI acts as a:

### Technical Systems Partner

### Backend Growth Partner

### Implementation Partner

We help agencies add and deliver services like:

* AI systems
* CRM automation
* Voice AI
* n8n workflows
* operational tooling
* multi-channel systems
* internal tools
* API integrations
* lead qualification systems
* automation-heavy workflows

Instead of building these capabilities internally, agencies can partner with FusionSync for implementation.

---

## Ideal Target

North Indian agencies that:

* work with US/Canada/UK clients
* already have active projects and delivery
* have 10+ clients or established operations
* build software, websites, dashboards, or business systems
* are likely getting requests for AI or automation work

Avoid:

* struggling solo agencies
* low-quality outsourcing shops
* agencies without real delivery maturity

---

## Offer

A **low-commitment pilot implementation**.

FusionSync offers to:

* build a small proof-of-concept
* create a custom demo
* implement a small internal use case
* help deliver one AI/system-related request for a client

The goal is to reduce friction and show execution quality before a deeper partnership.

Examples:

* internal agency workflow automation
* CRM automation
* AI workflow
* voice AI demo
* lead qualification system
* custom operational workflow
* client-requested AI feature

---

## Outreach Goal

Start conversations.

Build trust.

Show technical credibility.

Create long-term implementation relationships.

The ideal outcome is:

> agencies bring opportunities and clients
> FusionSync handles technical execution

while remaining a trusted backend implementation layer.
